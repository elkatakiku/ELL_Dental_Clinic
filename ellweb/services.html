Services Offered

ELL dental clinic offers the best treatment for you.

Oral Prophylaxis

A dental procedure comprises polishing and scaling to remove tartar and plaque build-up from the teeth' surfaces, including those between and beneath the gums.Its purpose is to lower the risk of gum disease and tooth decay.

Restoration

Cavity fillings, root canals, and even dental implants are examples of restorative procedures. Restorative dentistry has two goals: to restore the function of the teeth and to repair the visual appeal of the teeth.

Extraction

An extraction is the removal of a tooth beyond repair due to decay. A dental extraction may be required due to severe tooth decay, infection, or crowding.

Odontectomy

A dental surgical procedure is done on the impacted tooth. The process includes cutting into the gums to expose the affected tooth and the bone area covering the tooth.

Root Canal Treatment

A root canal removes the inflamed or diseased pulp from the interior of the tooth. A treatment to repair and save a badly damaged or infected tooth instead of removing it. The term "root canal" comes from cleaning of the canals inside a tooth's root.

Prosthodontics

Prosthodontics is the branch of dentistry that deals with repairing or replacing teeth using prostheses. Dental crowns and bridges, dentures, and even dental implants are included.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is a branch of dentistry that focuses on detecting, preventing, and repairing crooked teeth and jaws, as well as incorrect bite patterns.
