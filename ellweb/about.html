About Us

We Are ELL Dental Clinic

ELL Dental Clinic was founded by Dra. Edna Laurel Leonor
in the year 2008, the clinic was named after her. The Dental
Clinic is located in the 2nd floor of Lotus Mall, Nueno Avenue
Brgy. Tanzang Luma 1 Imus City, Cavite, where they offer
high-quality dental services for 13 years now. ELL Dental
Clinic comprises of four people; the owner, Dra. Edna Laurel
Leonor; two Associate Dentists, Dra. Alma Esagunda Cruz and
Dra. Agnes Alvarez, that has an alternating duty; and,
a dental secretary, Sec. Myrna Adaza, who maintains the clinic
and handles all the paper works.
