Services Offered

Here in ELL Dental Clinic, we offer the best treatment for you.

Oral Prophylaxis

Polishing and scaling to remove tartar and plaque build-up from the teeth's surfaces.

Restoration

Cavity fillings, root canals, dental implant, and many more.

Extraction

Removal of a tooth beyond repair due to decay.

Odontectomy

Dental surgical procedure on the impacted tooth.

Root Canal Treatment

Repairing and saving a badly damaged or infected tooth instead of removing it.

Prosthodontics

Repairing or replacing teeth using crowns and bridges, dentures, etc.

About Us

Extensive dental expertise, truly state-of-the-art, and the best locations in Manila. Dental World Manila embodies all these, with clients guaranteed to experience and enjoy every such aspect with every service. Headed by Dr. Malony L. Santos of St. Luke’s Medical Center, the Philippines’s foremost hospital, Dental World Manila is your hub for comprehensive and highly specialized dental treatments, such as cosmetic dentistry, dental implants,TMJ, and periodontics, among others.
